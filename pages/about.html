  • Dexter Moran

Dexter Moran is a designer with a passion for computational and experimental design. As a recent graduate of University of Illinois Chicago's Industrial Design program, he has a wide range of experience from consulting for a green tech start up to performing in a research role for a NASA grant solicitation. Dexter aims to design parametric products at a human scale, create accessible interfaces, and utilize technology to create meaningful outcomes.
